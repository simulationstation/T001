What Ryan likely cares about here

R_med = median(F_post / F_pre) from same-location forced photometry across scanned pairs, not just a single catalog hit.

S_diff,max = max(F_diff / sigma_diff) over the support set, with empirical star-based registration already baked into the strict rerun.

epsilon_reg is the median registration residual in pixels. High values hurt rank directly.

Branch-aware logic

The ranking favors persistent, same-location suppression with low rebrightening, low artifact risk, high forced-photometry coherence, and clean provenance.

It penalizes dipole-like behavior, centroid migration, weak coverage, chip-gap / edge ambiguity, and dust-survivor-like patterns.

These are not publication claims. They are strict detector survivors ranked by export-failure coherence under the current assumption-conditioned framework.
